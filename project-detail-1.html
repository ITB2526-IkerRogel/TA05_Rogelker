Game Development

Protocolo Palma
2D Metroidvania

Project Overview

Protocolo Palma is a 2D Metroidvania-style platformer developed as a student project at the Institut Tecnològic de Barcelona. The game combines exploration, combat, and puzzle-solving in a hostile alien environment.

Designed with a focus on environmental storytelling and progressive mechanics, the player must adapt to an unknown world to survive while mastering new abilities.

Synopsis

The story follows three friends: Saba, Jesús, and Iker. During a quiet afternoon in the park, an alien portal opens, and a biomechanical creature kidnaps Jesús, taking him to the mysterious Planet Vegeta to control his mind using an artificial intelligence known as the "Mind Core."

Armed with runic devices and guided by Elyra, a local rebel warrior, Iker and Saba must travel to this strange world, face its dangers, and defeat the alien guardians to rescue their friend before he loses his humanity forever.

Key Features

The core gameplay revolves around a fluid platforming system and combat utilizing elemental runic abilities such as fire, speed, and electricity. Players can switch between unique characters: Iker, who is agile and equipped with an alien bow; Saba, the heavy "tank" with high strength and a tech mace; and Jesús, who utilizes a wheelchair with an integrated cannon.

To progress, players must defeat three major guardians who protect the rescue runes: Gor'khan, the swamp beast; Astrael, the psychic illusionist; and T'Varak, the master of shadows. Each encounter requires mastering different combat strategies.

Technical Specifications

The project was built using the Godot Engine (GDScript), featuring completely original Pixel Art for all characters, backgrounds, and animations. We implemented a robust infrastructure by configuring a secure DMZ server using IPFire to host the game's web services safely.

Currently, the project is in active development with Sprints 1 and 2 completed. This phase includes the implementation of base mechanics, initial level design, and the core enemy AI behaviors.
